About

The Pearl District Art Association is a small non-profit committed to bringing awareness & support to Portland's great art & artists. please check our calendar to see where & when you can attend art focused events in Portland. Pearl District Art Association is proud to award a scholarship to a young art student of our city yearly. To see how to apply, nominate a student or to donate to our scholarship fund please see our Scholarship section. If you interested in being a part of the Pearl District Art Association, check out our Support section. To find us on social media & for contact information, see the bottom right of this page.
